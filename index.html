How FoodWise Works

Three simple steps to turn food waste into hope

1

Donate

Restaurants and event organizers post surplus food with pickup details and photos.

2

Verify

Student volunteers verify food quality and safety, earning points for their contribution.

3

Deliver

Delivery agents pick up verified food and deliver to NGOs and communities in need.
